DECISION RULES
Your self-coaching framework. Reference every Sunday after check-in.
Load Adjustment
🟢 Score 8–10 — Increase Load
  • Add 10–15min to one Z2 session (rotate which one each week)
  • Keep hard session targets identical — never change volume AND intensity same week
  • If Z2 pace improved AND score 8+, tighten one hard target by 5sec/km or 5W
🟡 Score 5–7 — Hold Load
  • Failed 1 hard session but Z2 fine → keep load, review session placement
  • Z2 drifting above zone → fix zone discipline before any increase
  • HRV trending down but sessions okay → cut one Z2 session by 20min
  • Motivation flat but body okay → assess life stress first, not training load
🔴 Score 0–4 — Reduce Load
  • Cut ALL Z2 sessions by 30% duration
  • Drop to 2 hard sessions — remove lowest quality one
  • Add one full rest day — no optional swim, actual rest
  • Two consecutive weeks below 5 = mandatory full deload (Z1/Z2 only, 50% volume)
Hard Session Stacking Rules
RuleWhy
Never two hard sessions on consecutive daysRecovery takes 36–48hrs minimum
Never 285W+ within 48hrs of another hard session285W = ~124% FTP — VO2 max, very high cost
Max 3 hard sessions per week in Blocks 1–2More creates cumulative debt faster than recovery
Hard session always followed by Z1/Z2 dayAdaptation happens in recovery, not the session
